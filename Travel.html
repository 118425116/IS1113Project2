New York

Last Summer I went to New York for 5 nights. This was my first time in the US, but it definitely won't be my last!
We stayed in the Royalton Hotel in Manhattan. This hotel was in the ideal location as Time Squares was
just a five minute walk.
The city was so different to anywhere I've ever been. There is something going on at every turn!

Five attractions I'd recommend:

  • Brooklyn Bridge
  • The Met Museum
  • Top of the Rock
  • Grand Central Station
  • Tiffanys


Paris

I went to Paris two years for a school trip. I absolutely adore this city. There's so much to see.
The city is rich with history, covered in beautiful building and a rich exterior. The atmosphere is relaxed,
there are people sitting outside cafes everywhere. This city is easily one of my favourite places I've ever been.

5 attractions I'd recommend:

  • Notre Dame
  • The Eiffel Tower (Especially at night!)
  • Boat Trip on the Siene River
  • The Palace of Versailles
  • The Louvre
